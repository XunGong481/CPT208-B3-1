Project Personas

Project Context: "WanderX" Application (XJTLU Smart Campus Tour)
Platform & Tech: Mobile, XR, Location-based, Tangible interaction.
*These personas were co-created using AI following the 10-80-10 rule based on our project brief.

Persona 1: The Disoriented Freshman

Target: New Student Context: Onsite

Name: Chen Yuxuan

Age: 18 | Major: Computer Science

"XJTLU is massive, and the building layouts (like SA and FB) feel like a maze. I want to point my phone's camera at a building and see WanderX's AR arrows guiding me exactly to my lecture hall."

Goals:

  • Quickly navigate the campus onsite without getting lost.
  • Use mobile XR (AR navigation) for intuitive, real-time directional guidance based on his current location.

Persona 2: The Evaluating Parent

Target: External Visitor Context: Online / Onsite

Name: Sarah Lin

Age: 45 | Occupation: High School Teacher

"Before committing to a visit, I want an immersive online VR tour via WanderX to see if XJTLU's labs and dorms meet our expectations."

Goals:

  • Experience a realistic sense of the campus remotely through an Online XR mode.
  • Easily find visitor amenities (parking, cafes) using location-based services when visiting onsite.
