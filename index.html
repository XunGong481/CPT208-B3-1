Project Personas

Project Context: "XJTLU Campus Tour" Application
Platform & Tech: Mobile, XR, Location-based, Tangible interaction.

Persona 1: The Disoriented Freshman

Target: New Student Context: Onsite

Name: Chen Yuxuan

Age: 18 | Major: Computer Science

"XJTLU is massive, and the building layouts (like SA and FB) feel like a maze. I want to point my phone's camera at a building and see AR arrows guiding me exactly to my lecture hall."

Goals:

  • Quickly navigate the campus onsite without getting lost or being late for classes.
  • Use mobile XR (AR navigation) for intuitive, real-time directional guidance based on his current location.
  • Interact with physical campus landmarks (Tangible) by scanning them to unlock fun facts.

Frustrations:

  • Traditional 2D paper maps or standard map apps fail to show complex multi-floor building interiors.
  • Feeling anxious and out of place during the first few weeks of university life.

Persona 2: The Evaluating Parent

Target: External Visitor Context: Online / Onsite

Name: Sarah Lin

Age: 45 | Occupation: High School Teacher

"We live in another province. Before committing to a visit, I want an immersive online VR tour to see if XJTLU's labs and dorms meet our expectations."

Goals:

  • Experience a realistic sense of the campus atmosphere remotely through an Online XR mode.
  • When visiting onsite, use a self-guided mobile AR tour that acts as a virtual guide, explaining the history and function of buildings.
  • Easily find visitor amenities (parking, cafes, restrooms) using location-based services.

Frustrations:

  • Static photos on the university website don't provide a true sense of scale or the "vibe" of the campus.
  • When visiting onsite without a student guide, she misses out on the context and stories behind the campus architecture.
